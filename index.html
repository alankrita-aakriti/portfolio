FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete an order? +
Most orders take 5–15 working days depending on complexity. Rush orders (3–5 days) are available at a small additional charge. Timeline is confirmed before payment.
What quality of photo should I send? +
Clear, high-resolution, front-facing and well-lit photos work best for portraits. I'll let you know if the photo quality might affect the outcome.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes! I ship Pan-India and internationally. Domestic shipping is free for orders above ₹1000. International shipping charges vary by destination and will be shared upfront.
Can I request revisions? +
One free revision round is included with every order. Additional revisions may have a small charge. All discussed openly via WhatsApp.
What payment methods do you accept? +
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and PayPal (international). 50% advance on confirmation, remaining 50% before delivery.
Where can I see more of your work? +
Visit instagram.com/alankrita_aakriti to see the full portfolio. Feel free to DM there or WhatsApp for any enquiries.
